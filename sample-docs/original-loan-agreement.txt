BUSINESS LOAN AGREEMENT

This Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2025, between:

LENDER: Capital Financial Services LLC
Address: 123 Wall Street, New York, NY 10005

BORROWER: TechStart Industries Inc.
Address: 456 Innovation Drive, San Francisco, CA 94102

WHEREAS, Borrower has requested that Lender provide a loan facility, and Lender has agreed to provide such facility on the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

Section 1. Definitions and Interpretation

1.1 Principal Amount: The sum of Five Million Dollars ($5,000,000.00) to be advanced by Lender to Borrower.

1.2 Business Day: Any day other than Saturday, Sunday, or a day on which banking institutions in New York are authorized or required by law to close.

1.3 Default: Any Event of Default as defined in Section 7 of this Agreement.

Section 2. The Loan

2.1 Commitment: Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Lender agrees to lend to Borrower the Principal Amount.

2.2 Purpose: The loan proceeds shall be used solely for working capital and general corporate purposes.

2.3 Disbursement: The Principal Amount shall be disbursed to Borrower within five (5) Business Days following execution of this Agreement.

Section 3. Interest Rate and Payments

3.1 Interest Rate: The outstanding principal balance shall bear interest at a fixed rate of seven percent (7.00%) per annum.

3.2 Payment Schedule: Borrower shall make monthly payments of principal and interest commencing on March 1, 2025, and continuing on the first day of each month thereafter.

3.3 Calculation: Interest shall be calculated on the basis of actual days elapsed and a 360-day year.

3.4 Late Payment Fee: If any payment is not received within ten (10) days of its due date, Borrower shall pay a late fee equal to five percent (5%) of the overdue amount.

Section 4. Maturity and Prepayment

4.1 Maturity Date: The entire outstanding principal balance, together with all accrued and unpaid interest, shall be due and payable on January 15, 2030 (the "Maturity Date").

4.2 Prepayment: Borrower may prepay the loan in whole or in part at any time without penalty or premium.

4.3 Application of Prepayments: Any prepayments shall be applied first to accrued interest and then to principal.

Section 5. Collateral and Security

5.1 Security Interest: To secure the payment and performance of all obligations under this Agreement, Borrower hereby grants to Lender a first priority security interest in all of Borrower's accounts receivable, inventory, and equipment.

5.2 Perfection: Borrower agrees to execute and deliver such documents as Lender may reasonably request to perfect its security interest.

Section 6. Representations and Warranties

6.1 Organization: Borrower is a corporation duly organized, validly existing, and in good standing under the laws of Delaware.

6.2 Authority: Borrower has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement and to perform its obligations hereunder.

6.3 Financial Statements: All financial statements provided by Borrower to Lender are true, accurate, and complete in all material respects.

6.4 No Litigation: There is no action, suit, or proceeding pending or threatened against Borrower that could materially adversely affect its business or financial condition.

Section 7. Covenants

7.1 Financial Reporting: Borrower shall provide to Lender:
   (a) Annual audited financial statements within 120 days after fiscal year end
   (b) Quarterly unaudited financial statements within 45 days after quarter end

7.2 Minimum EBITDA: Borrower shall maintain minimum EBITDA of at least $1,000,000 per fiscal quarter, tested quarterly.

7.3 Debt-to-Equity Ratio: Borrower shall maintain a debt-to-equity ratio not exceeding 2.0:1.0, tested quarterly.

7.4 Capital Expenditures: Borrower shall not make capital expenditures exceeding $500,000 in any fiscal year without Lender's prior written consent.

7.5 Dividends: Borrower may pay dividends to its shareholders, provided that no Event of Default has occurred and is continuing.

7.6 Additional Debt: Borrower shall not incur additional debt exceeding $250,000 without Lender's prior written consent.

Section 8. Events of Default

8.1 The following shall constitute Events of Default:

   (a) Failure to pay principal or interest when due and such failure continues for fifteen (15) days;
   
   (b) Breach of any covenant contained in Section 7 and such breach continues for thirty (30) days after written notice;
   
   (c) Any representation or warranty proves to have been materially incorrect when made;
   
   (d) Borrower becomes insolvent or commences bankruptcy proceedings;
   
   (e) A judgment exceeding $100,000 is entered against Borrower and remains unsatisfied for sixty (60) days.

Section 9. Remedies

9.1 Acceleration: Upon the occurrence of an Event of Default, Lender may declare all outstanding amounts immediately due and payable.

9.2 Enforcement of Security: Lender may exercise any and all rights and remedies available under the Uniform Commercial Code.

Section 10. Fees and Expenses

10.1 Origination Fee: Borrower shall pay Lender an origination fee of one percent (1.0%) of the Principal Amount, payable at closing.

10.2 Legal Fees: Borrower shall reimburse Lender for all reasonable legal fees and expenses incurred in connection with this Agreement.

Section 11. General Provisions

11.1 Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

11.2 Notices: All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses set forth above.

11.3 Amendments: This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both parties.

11.4 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings.

11.5 Severability: If any provision is held invalid, the remainder shall continue in full force and effect.
