COMMERCIAL LOAN AGREEMENT

This Commercial Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is made as of March 3, 2025, by and between:

LENDER: Northgate Bank, N.A.
Address: 800 Market Street, Chicago, IL 60606

BORROWER: Harbor Logistics, LLC
Address: 2200 Riverfront Blvd, St. Louis, MO 63101

RECITALS
Borrower has requested a term loan, and Lender agrees to extend credit on the terms below.

Section 1. Loan Amount and Purpose
1.1 Loan Amount: Three Million Dollars ($3,000,000).
1.2 Purpose: Fleet upgrades and working capital.

Section 2. Interest and Payments
2.1 Interest Rate: Fixed at 6.25% per annum.
2.2 Payment Schedule: Monthly principal and interest, beginning May 1, 2025.
2.3 Late Charge: 4% of any payment not received within 10 days.

Section 3. Maturity
3.1 Maturity Date: March 3, 2030.

Section 4. Prepayment
4.1 Prepayment: Permitted without penalty.

Section 5. Collateral
5.1 Collateral: First-priority lien on all vehicles and related equipment.

Section 6. Financial Covenants
6.1 Minimum Liquidity: Borrower must maintain at least $400,000 in unrestricted cash.
6.2 Leverage Ratio: Total Debt / EBITDA not to exceed 2.75x, tested quarterly.

Section 7. Reporting
7.1 Financial Statements: Quarterly unaudited statements within 45 days.
7.2 Annual Audited Statements: Within 120 days of fiscal year end.

Section 8. Events of Default
8.1 Payment Default: Failure to pay within 15 days of due date.
8.2 Covenant Default: Failure to cure within 30 days after notice.

Section 9. Fees
9.1 Origination Fee: 0.75% of Loan Amount.

Section 10. Governing Law
10.1 Governing Law: Illinois.
