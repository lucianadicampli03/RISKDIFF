AMENDED AND RESTATED BUSINESS LOAN AGREEMENT

This Amended and Restated Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of August 1, 2025, between:

LENDER: Capital Financial Services LLC
Address: 123 Wall Street, New York, NY 10005

BORROWER: TechStart Industries Inc.
Address: 456 Innovation Drive, San Francisco, CA 94102

WHEREAS, Borrower and Lender entered into a Business Loan Agreement dated January 15, 2025 (the "Original Agreement"); and

WHEREAS, the parties desire to amend and restate the Original Agreement in its entirety as set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

Section 1. Definitions and Interpretation

1.1 Principal Amount: The sum of Five Million Dollars ($5,000,000.00) to be advanced by Lender to Borrower.

1.2 Business Day: Any day other than Saturday, Sunday, or a day on which banking institutions in New York are authorized or required by law to close.

1.3 Default: Any Event of Default as defined in Section 7 of this Agreement.

1.4 Material Adverse Effect: Any event, circumstance, or condition that has or could reasonably be expected to have a material adverse effect on the business, operations, properties, assets, or financial condition of Borrower.

Section 2. The Loan

2.1 Commitment: Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Lender agrees to lend to Borrower the Principal Amount.

2.2 Purpose: The loan proceeds shall be used solely for working capital, general corporate purposes, and approved acquisitions as defined in Section 7.8.

2.3 Disbursement: The Principal Amount shall be disbursed to Borrower within five (5) Business Days following execution of this Agreement.

Section 3. Interest Rate and Payments

3.1 Interest Rate: The outstanding principal balance shall bear interest at a variable rate equal to the Secured Overnight Financing Rate (SOFR) plus 350 basis points (3.50%), adjusted monthly.

3.2 Interest Rate Floor: Notwithstanding Section 3.1, the interest rate shall not be less than six percent (6.00%) per annum.

3.3 Payment Schedule: Borrower shall make monthly payments of interest only commencing on March 1, 2025, and continuing on the first day of each month thereafter, with all principal due at maturity.

3.4 Calculation: Interest shall be calculated on the basis of actual days elapsed and a 360-day year.

3.5 Late Payment Fee: If any payment is not received within ten (10) days of its due date, Borrower shall pay a late fee equal to five percent (5%) of the overdue amount plus reasonable collection costs incurred by Lender.

Section 4. Maturity and Prepayment

4.1 Maturity Date: The entire outstanding principal balance, together with all accrued and unpaid interest, shall be due and payable on January 15, 2028 (the "Maturity Date").

4.2 Prepayment: Borrower may prepay the loan in whole or in part at any time, subject to a prepayment penalty of two percent (2.0%) of the amount prepaid if prepayment occurs within the first twenty-four (24) months.

4.3 Application of Prepayments: Any prepayments shall be applied first to fees and expenses, then to accrued interest, and finally to principal.

Section 5. Collateral and Security

5.1 Security Interest: To secure the payment and performance of all obligations under this Agreement, Borrower hereby grants to Lender a first priority security interest in all of Borrower's assets, including but not limited to accounts receivable, inventory, equipment, intellectual property, and general intangibles.

5.2 Perfection: Borrower agrees to execute and deliver such documents as Lender may reasonably request to perfect its security interest.

5.3 Personal Guarantee: The Chief Executive Officer and Chief Financial Officer of Borrower shall provide unlimited personal guarantees of all obligations under this Agreement.

Section 6. Representations and Warranties

6.1 Organization: Borrower is a corporation duly organized, validly existing, and in good standing under the laws of Delaware.

6.2 Authority: Borrower has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement and to perform its obligations hereunder.

6.3 Financial Statements: All financial statements provided by Borrower to Lender are true, accurate, and complete in all material respects.

6.4 No Litigation: There is no action, suit, or proceeding pending or threatened against Borrower that could materially adversely affect its business or financial condition.

6.5 Compliance with Laws: Borrower is in compliance with all applicable federal, state, and local laws, regulations, and ordinances.

Section 7. Covenants

7.1 Financial Reporting: Borrower shall provide to Lender:
   (a) Annual audited financial statements within 90 days after fiscal year end
   (b) Quarterly unaudited financial statements within 30 days after quarter end
   (c) Monthly management reports within 15 days after month end

7.2 Minimum EBITDA: Borrower shall maintain minimum EBITDA of at least $1,500,000 per fiscal quarter, tested quarterly.

7.3 Debt Service Coverage Ratio: Borrower shall maintain a debt service coverage ratio of at least 1.25:1.0, tested quarterly.

7.4 Current Ratio: Borrower shall maintain a current ratio of at least 1.5:1.0, tested quarterly.

7.5 Capital Expenditures: Borrower shall not make capital expenditures exceeding $300,000 in any fiscal year without Lender's prior written consent.

7.6 Dividends: Borrower shall not pay dividends to its shareholders without Lender's prior written consent.

7.7 Additional Debt: Borrower shall not incur any additional debt without Lender's prior written consent.

7.8 Acquisitions: Borrower shall not acquire any business or entity without Lender's prior written consent, which consent shall not be unreasonably withheld for acquisitions not exceeding $500,000.

7.9 Insurance: Borrower shall maintain comprehensive insurance coverage satisfactory to Lender, including business interruption insurance.

Section 8. Events of Default

8.1 The following shall constitute Events of Default:

   (a) Failure to pay principal or interest when due and such failure continues for five (5) days;
   
   (b) Breach of any covenant contained in Section 7 and such breach continues for fifteen (15) days after written notice;
   
   (c) Any representation or warranty proves to have been materially incorrect when made;
   
   (d) Borrower becomes insolvent or commences bankruptcy proceedings;
   
   (e) A judgment exceeding $50,000 is entered against Borrower and remains unsatisfied for thirty (30) days;
   
   (f) Any Material Adverse Effect occurs;
   
   (g) Change of control of Borrower occurs without Lender's prior written consent.

Section 9. Remedies

9.1 Acceleration: Upon the occurrence of an Event of Default, Lender may immediately declare all outstanding amounts due and payable without notice or cure period.

9.2 Enforcement of Security: Lender may exercise any and all rights and remedies available under the Uniform Commercial Code or applicable law.

9.3 Default Interest Rate: Upon the occurrence of an Event of Default, the interest rate shall increase by an additional two percent (2.0%) per annum.

Section 10. Fees and Expenses

10.1 Origination Fee: Borrower shall pay Lender an origination fee of one percent (1.0%) of the Principal Amount, payable at closing.

10.2 Annual Agency Fee: Borrower shall pay Lender an annual agency fee of $15,000, payable in advance on each anniversary of this Agreement.

10.3 Legal Fees: Borrower shall reimburse Lender for all reasonable legal fees and expenses incurred in connection with this Agreement, including enforcement actions.

10.4 Audit Fees: Borrower shall reimburse Lender for reasonable costs of field audits, not to exceed two audits per year unless an Event of Default has occurred.

Section 11. General Provisions

11.1 Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

11.2 Notices: All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses set forth above or by email to designated representatives.

11.3 Amendments: This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both parties.

11.4 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, including the Original Agreement.

11.5 Severability: If any provision is held invalid, the remainder shall continue in full force and effect.

11.6 Waiver of Jury Trial: Both parties hereby irrevocably waive any right to trial by jury in any action arising out of this Agreement.
