AMENDED AND RESTATED REAL ESTATE TERM LOAN AGREEMENT

This Amended and Restated Real Estate Term Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is dated February 14, 2026, between:

LENDER: Summit Regional Bank
Address: 900 Pine Street, Seattle, WA 98101

BORROWER: Evergreen Office Partners, LP
Address: 455 Lakeview Avenue, Seattle, WA 98102

Section 1. Loan Amount and Use
1.1 Loan Amount: Ten Million Dollars ($10,000,000).
1.2 Use of Proceeds: Acquisition and capital improvements to 455 Lakeview Avenue.

Section 2. Interest Rate and Payments
2.1 Interest Rate: Variable at SOFR + 2.75%, adjusted quarterly.
2.2 Interest Rate Cap: Not to exceed 8.50% per annum.
2.3 Payments: Interest-only through December 2026; thereafter principal and interest.

Section 3. Maturity
3.1 Maturity Date: May 20, 2031.

Section 4. Prepayment
4.1 Prepayment: Allowed with a 2% penalty in years 1-3, 1% in years 4-5.

Section 5. Collateral
5.1 Collateral: First deed of trust on the property, assignment of rents, and pledge of borrower equity interests.

Section 6. Financial Covenants
6.1 DSCR: Minimum 1.40x, tested quarterly.
6.2 Debt Yield: Minimum 10.0%, tested annually.
6.3 Net Operating Income: Minimum $1,050,000 annually.

Section 7. Reserves
7.1 Capital Reserves: $250,000 annually.
7.2 Leasing Reserves: $125,000 annually.
7.3 Tenant Improvement Reserves: $100,000 annually.

Section 8. Insurance
8.1 Insurance: Expanded coverage including business interruption insurance.

Section 9. Default
9.1 Default: Payment default after 5 days; covenant default after 15 days; cash sweep on DSCR breach.

Section 10. Fees
10.1 Origination Fee: 0.75% of Loan Amount.
10.2 Asset Management Fee: $20,000 per year.

Section 11. Governing Law
11.1 Governing Law: Washington.
