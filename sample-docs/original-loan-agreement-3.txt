REAL ESTATE TERM LOAN AGREEMENT

This Real Estate Term Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is dated May 20, 2024, between:

LENDER: Summit Regional Bank
Address: 900 Pine Street, Seattle, WA 98101

BORROWER: Evergreen Office Partners, LP
Address: 455 Lakeview Avenue, Seattle, WA 98102

Section 1. Loan Amount and Use
1.1 Loan Amount: Ten Million Dollars ($10,000,000).
1.2 Use of Proceeds: Acquisition of 455 Lakeview Avenue office property.

Section 2. Interest Rate and Payments
2.1 Interest Rate: Fixed at 5.90% per annum.
2.2 Payments: Monthly principal and interest, beginning July 1, 2024.

Section 3. Maturity
3.1 Maturity Date: May 20, 2034.

Section 4. Prepayment
4.1 Prepayment: Allowed after year two with 1% penalty.

Section 5. Collateral
5.1 Collateral: First deed of trust on the property and assignment of rents.

Section 6. Financial Covenants
6.1 DSCR: Minimum 1.30x, tested quarterly.
6.2 Debt Yield: Minimum 9.0%, tested annually.

Section 7. Reserves
7.1 Capital Reserves: $150,000 annually.
7.2 Leasing Reserves: $75,000 annually.

Section 8. Insurance
8.1 Insurance: Property and liability coverage per lender requirements.

Section 9. Default
9.1 Default: Payment default after 15 days; covenant default after 30 days.

Section 10. Fees
10.1 Origination Fee: 0.50% of Loan Amount.

Section 11. Governing Law
11.1 Governing Law: Washington.
