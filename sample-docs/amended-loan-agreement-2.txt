AMENDED AND RESTATED COMMERCIAL LOAN AGREEMENT

This Amended and Restated Commercial Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is made as of October 10, 2025, by and between:

LENDER: Northgate Bank, N.A.
Address: 800 Market Street, Chicago, IL 60606

BORROWER: Harbor Logistics, LLC
Address: 2200 Riverfront Blvd, St. Louis, MO 63101

RECITALS
Borrower and Lender entered into a Commercial Loan Agreement dated March 3, 2025. The parties now amend and restate that Agreement.

Section 1. Loan Amount and Purpose
1.1 Loan Amount: Three Million Dollars ($3,000,000).
1.2 Purpose: Fleet upgrades, working capital, and warehouse automation initiatives.

Section 2. Interest and Payments
2.1 Interest Rate: Variable at SOFR + 3.25%, adjusted monthly.
2.2 Interest Rate Floor: Not less than 5.75% per annum.
2.3 Payment Schedule: Interest-only payments monthly through April 2026; thereafter principal and interest.
2.4 Late Charge: 5% of any payment not received within 5 days.

Section 3. Maturity
3.1 Maturity Date: March 3, 2028.

Section 4. Prepayment
4.1 Prepayment: Permitted with a 2% penalty if prepaid within 18 months.

Section 5. Collateral
5.1 Collateral: First-priority lien on all vehicles, equipment, and accounts receivable.
5.2 Additional Security: Corporate guarantee by Harbor Holdings, Inc.

Section 6. Financial Covenants
6.1 Minimum Liquidity: Borrower must maintain at least $600,000 in unrestricted cash.
6.2 Leverage Ratio: Total Debt / EBITDA not to exceed 2.25x, tested quarterly.
6.3 Fixed Charge Coverage: Minimum 1.20x, tested quarterly.

Section 7. Reporting
7.1 Financial Statements: Monthly unaudited statements within 20 days.
7.2 Annual Audited Statements: Within 90 days of fiscal year end.

Section 8. Events of Default
8.1 Payment Default: Failure to pay within 5 days of due date.
8.2 Covenant Default: Failure to cure within 15 days after notice.
8.3 Material Adverse Change.

Section 9. Fees
9.1 Origination Fee: 1.00% of Loan Amount.
9.2 Annual Agency Fee: $12,000 per year.

Section 10. Governing Law
10.1 Governing Law: Illinois.
